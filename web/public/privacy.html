🍔 MacroPro — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 July 2026

The short version: MacroPro has no accounts and no backend that stores your data. The macros you enter stay in your browser, and the optional browser extension works only through your own, already-signed-in MyFitnessPal session. We never see or store your MyFitnessPal password, and we don't collect, track, or sell any personal information.

Who this covers

This policy applies to the MacroPro web app at macropro.food and the optional MacroPro MyFitnessPal Companion browser extension.

What we don't collect

  • No sign-up, no user accounts, no profiles.
  • No servers that receive or store the macros, meals, or restaurants you choose.
  • No analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies.
  • No selling or sharing of data with third parties — because we don't collect it.

Macros you enter

Target macros you type (or pull from MyFitnessPal) are processed entirely in your browser to compute meal suggestions. They are not sent to any MacroPro server. When you choose to log a meal, the macro totals may be placed on your clipboard or passed to your MyFitnessPal tab so they can be filled into MyFitnessPal — never to us.

Nutrition data

Restaurant nutrition figures are pre-generated static files the app downloads to run the optimizer in your browser. They contain no personal data and are the same for every visitor.

MyFitnessPal integration

MyFitnessPal has no public API for this, so MacroPro connects two ways, both of which run inside your own browser using your existing MyFitnessPal login:

  • Bookmarklets — small snippets you run on your open MyFitnessPal tab that read your "Remaining" macros or fill the Quick Add form.
  • Browser extension (optional) — does the same in one click.

In both cases your MyFitnessPal session cookies and password never leave your browser and are never transmitted to MacroPro. MacroPro stores none of it.

Browser extension permissions

The extension requests only what these two actions need, and uses them for nothing else:

  • host_permissions for myfitnesspal.com — to read your diary's "Remaining" row and to fill/submit the Quick Add form on your own session.
  • scripting — to inject the small reader/filler script into your open MyFitnessPal tab. It runs only on the MacroPro app pages and on myfitnesspal.com.
  • tabs — to find your existing MyFitnessPal tab (or open the food diary) so actions run on the right page.

The extension collects no data, sets no cookies, and sends nothing to any server. It acts only when you click a button in MacroPro.

Hosting

The site is served as static files by Cloudflare, which may process standard request metadata (such as IP address) to deliver and protect the site, per Cloudflare's own policies. MacroPro does not add any tracking on top of this.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change. Material changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

MacroPro is a free, open project. Macros and credentials never leave your browser.
